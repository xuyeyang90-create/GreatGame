# Game Brief

## High concept

Run a clandestine bio-lab. Manage adult bio-engineered operatives, send female operatives on multi-day infiltration and companionship missions, earn funding and authorized paternal samples, and breed future generations while balancing bloodline quality, loyalty, health, aging, and exposure risk.

## Player fantasy

The player should feel like:
- a lab director making hard tradeoffs
- a bloodline planner, not just a card collector
- someone choosing between short-term profit and long-term sustainability

## Main loss condition

Exposure risk reaches 100.

## Main strategic tensions

- earn money now vs preserve key operatives
- use a top operative again vs protect her loyalty and health
- breed now vs wait for safer conditions
- chase elite bloodlines vs avoid hidden defects
- preserve a stable line vs gamble on volatile talent

## Main card groups

### Female cards
- can infiltrate
- can become pregnant
- have stamina, health, loyalty, age, life stage, and traits

### Male cards
- used mainly as fathers
- have stamina, health, age, traits, and bloodline value
- do not infiltrate

### Retrieved sample cards
- external paternal sources brought back from missions
- can substitute for a father in breeding
- carry source quality, tags, and limited usage / quality logic

## Main visible stats

- appearance
- figure
- aura
- gentleness
- eloquence
- stability
- constitution
- stamina
- health
- age
- life stage
- loyalty (female only)

## Main hidden simulation

- multi-locus genotype for visible stats
- breeding gene group
- hidden defect loci
- latent trait triggers
- bloodline tags
- aging wear
- protocol stability

## Tone constraints

- adult operatives only
- no explicit sexual content
- mission writing stays suggestive / social / intelligence oriented rather than explicit
- keep the prototype systemic and readable
